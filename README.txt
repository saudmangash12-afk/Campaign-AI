Campaign AI
Marketing Intelligence Simulator

----------------------------------------
Overview
----------------------------------------
Campaign AI is a performance-driven marketing intelligence simulator designed to analyze and optimize digital advertising campaigns across multiple platforms.

The system evaluates campaign performance using key marketing KPIs such as CTR, CPM, Conversion Rate, CPA, and ROI to support data-driven decision making.

----------------------------------------
Key Features
----------------------------------------
- Multi-platform campaign analysis (TikTok, Instagram, Snapchat, Facebook, LinkedIn)
- Audience targeting based on age segmentation
- Campaign objective modeling (Awareness, Traffic, Leads, Sales)
- KPI-based performance evaluation and optimization
- Automated platform scoring and ranking engine
- Interactive data visualization dashboard
- Campaign history tracking
- Exportable reports (Word format)

----------------------------------------
Business Use Case
----------------------------------------
Campaign AI simulates real-world marketing decision workflows used by digital marketing teams to:

- Compare platform performance
- Allocate budgets efficiently
- Optimize campaign strategies
- Improve ROI and conversion outcomes
